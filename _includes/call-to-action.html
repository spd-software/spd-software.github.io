Our Agile software development process is designed based on the fundamentals of Agile methodology. Our software engineer teams apply the practices of Agile frameworks, including Scrum, Kanban.


Manifesto for Agile Software Development become the compass for our engineers


We are uncovering better ways of developing
software by doing it and helping others do it.
Through this work we have come to value:

Individuals and interactions over processes and tools
 Working software over comprehensive documentation
 Customer collaboration over contract negotiation
 Responding to change over following a plan

That is, while there is value in the items on
the right, we value the items on the left more.
